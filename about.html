ABOUT SCHOOL

School Imformations

To pursue an excellence in teaching, research and community engagement in Engineering Education to address the challenges of ever-changing society and globalization


Mission

Deliver Comprehensive and Dynamic

- Education: Covers core engineering principles, advanced topics, and interdisciplinary studies to build a solid foundation of knowledge and skills.

- Foster advancement in research: Promotes Innovation, collaboration and ethical practices; and to encourage faculty and students to think outside the box by integrating project-based learning, creative thinking methodologies, and opportunities to tackle real-world challenges; and Facilitates access to research projects, internships, and industry collaboration to give practical experience and exposure to cutting-edge technologies and practices.

- Cultivate a Global Perspective: Prepare students for the global engineering landscape by offering international exchange programs and fostering collaborations with global institutions.

Enhance Soft Skills: Develop students' communication, teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving abilities through group projects, presentations, and leadership roles within student organizations.

- Encourage Lifelong Learning: Promote continuous education and professional development by encouraging students to stay abreast of emerging technologies, trends, and best practices in engineering.

- Engage with the Community: Involve students in community service and outreach programs to help them understand the societal impact of their work and develop a sense of social responsibility.

To pursue an excellence in teaching, research and community engagement in Engineering Education to address the challenges of ever-changing society and globalization
